# UX-006 — Loading, Empty & Error States

Document ID: UX-006

Version: 1.0

Status: Approved

---

# Philosophy

No state should feel broken.

Every state should educate or reassure the user.

---

# Loading

Never show generic spinners.

Use contextual progress.

Example

Analyzing Skills...

Checking ATS Compatibility...

Estimating Salary...

Building Career IQ...

This transforms waiting into anticipation.

---

# Empty States

Resume History

"No resumes yet."

CTA

Upload Resume

---

Achievements

"No achievements yet."

CTA

Complete your first mission.

---

Career Roadmap

"No roadmap available."

CTA

Finish your first analysis.

---

# Error States

Resume Upload Failed

Explain why.

Offer Retry.

Offer Help.

Never blame the user.

---

AI Failure

Show

"We couldn't complete your analysis this time."

Provide

Retry

Contact Support

Status Page

---

Network Failure

Display offline banner.

Preserve user work.

Retry automatically.

---

# Success States

Career IQ Increased

Celebrate briefly.

Do not interrupt workflow.

---

Mission Completed

Small celebration.

Confetti only for major milestones.

---

Resume Improved

Highlight differences.

Show measurable impact.

---

# Skeleton Loading

Use skeleton components instead of blank spaces.

Maintain page layout.

Prevent content shifting.

---

# Empty Screen Rule

Every empty screen answers

"What should I do next?"

---

# Error Rule

Every error provides

Reason

Recovery

Next Action

Never leave users at a dead end.
