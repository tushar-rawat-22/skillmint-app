# COMPANY-001: SkillMint Doctrine

## What SkillMint Is

SkillMint is a proof-aware career operating system for students, freshers, and early-career professionals.

It helps users convert their current resume, skills, projects, and proof into:

- Career clarity
- Role-fit direction
- Job readiness
- Proof-building roadmap
- Application confidence

## What SkillMint Is Not

SkillMint is not:

- Just a resume analyzer
- Just an ATS checker
- Just an AI resume writer
- Just a job tracker
- Just a roadmap generator
- Just a score dashboard

Those are features, not the company.

## Core Belief

Most students do not know:

- Which jobs to apply for
- Which roles their resume actually fits
- Which claims are weak
- Which skills are missing
- What proof recruiters need
- What to build next

SkillMint exists to solve this clarity gap.

## Core Promise

SkillMint first discovers where your resume currently fits, then checks how trustworthy your claims are, then compares you against real jobs, then gives you a roadmap to close the gap.

## Product Loop

```text
Upload resume
-> Resume Reality
-> Profile-fit roles
-> Active Target
-> Proof Confidence
-> Career IQ
-> Latest JD Match
-> Roadmap / Missions
-> Improve proof
-> Re-score
```

SkillMint sells:
