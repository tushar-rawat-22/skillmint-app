# SkillMint Beta Tester Script

> **Status: Draft outreach script only.** Public or external beta outreach is not authorized until the release blockers in [PROJECT_STATUS.md](PROJECT_STATUS.md) and [DEPLOYMENT.md](DEPLOYMENT.md) are closed.

## Message To Send Testers

Hey, I am testing SkillMint with a small group before inviting more users. It is a proof-aware career operating system for students and freshers. It reads your resume, shows where your profile stands, what roles fit your current signals, what proof is missing, and what to fix next.

This is a beta, so I am not looking for politeness. I want to know what felt useful, fake, confusing, or untrustworthy.

Time needed: about 10 minutes.

## 10-Minute Test Script

1. Create an account or sign in.
2. Upload your current resume.
3. Open the dashboard and read Career IQ, Proof Confidence, Harsh Truth, and profile-fit roles.
4. Open Resume and check saved analyses / active report language.
5. Open ATS Match and paste one real job description.
6. Read the Latest JD Match and the recommendations.
7. Optional: open Roadmap and see whether the next steps feel useful.
8. Submit feedback.

## Feedback Questions

- In one sentence, what do you think SkillMint does?
- What felt useful?
- What felt fake or confusing?
- What score did you not trust?
- Did you understand Career IQ?
- Did you understand Proof Confidence?
- Could you tell profile-fit role from Latest JD Match?
- Could you tell saved analysis from active report?
- Would you come back after improving your resume? Why or why not?
- What would make this worth paying for later?
